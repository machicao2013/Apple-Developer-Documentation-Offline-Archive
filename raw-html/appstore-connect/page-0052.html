Edit access to an app

You can limit app access to specific people within your App Store Connect account if they have the App Manager, Customer Support, Developer, Marketing, or Sales (with no access to reports) roles.

Learn about managing appâ€™s user access using the App Store Connect API.

Required role: Account Holder or Admin. View role permissions.

  1. In Apps, select the app you want to view.

  2. In the sidebar, under General click App Information.

  3. Scroll to the Additional Information section and click Edit User Access.

  4. Choose Limited Access or Full Access. If you select Full Access, all users will have access to the app. Users with the Admin or Finance roles, as well as those with access to reports, will always have access to all apps.

  5. Select the users who should have access to the app, then click Save.
