Manage agreements

View agreements status

You can view Paid Applications Agreement’s terms, statuses, and expiration dates, as well as enter missing information and download PDF copies.

Note: Vew and download the Free Apps Agreement, also known as the Apple Developer Program License Agreement, in Agreements and Guidelines for Apple Developers. You can also download it from the Membership section of your developer account by clicking Show Agreements.

Required role: Account Holder, Admin, or Finance. View role permissions.

View the status of an agreement

The agreement status indicates whether your agreement is active or if it needs your attention.

  1. In the homepage, click Business.

  2. On the Agreements tab, find the agreement you want to view. The status appears in the corresponding row.

    Status

    Description

    New

    The agreement hasn't been signed.

    Pending User Info

    You’ve signed an agreement, but it’s not in effect because you haven’t finished providing required information.

    Processing

    You’ve provided all the information and we’re reviewing your agreement.

    Verifying

    You’ve provided additional information about the legal entity associated with the agreement and the details are being reviewed.

    Active

    The agreement is in effect.

    Active (Pending User)

    The agreement is in effect, but you haven’t finished providing required information.

    Active (New Agreement Available)

    A new version of the existing active agreement is available for you to sign.

    Pending (New Legal Entity)

    You’ve made changes to your legal entity and you're required to sign a new contract using the updated legal entity.

    Pending (Update Legal Entity)

    You’ve made changes to your legal entity and new banking and/or tax forms are required.

    Pending (New Agreement Available)

    A new version of the existing pending agreement is available for you to sign.

    Expired

    The agreement has expired.

    Disabled

    The Agreement is disabled, and your app is removed from the App Store until you provide the required information and the information is accepted by Apple.

Download a copy of an agreement

You can download a copy of your agreements at any time in App Store Connect.

  1. In the homepage, click Business.

  2. On the Agreements tab, click the agreement you want to download.

    Note: You can also click the download icon to the right of the agreement to download.

  3. Click Download at the top right corner of the prompt.
