Manage In-App Purchases

View and edit In-App Purchase information

You can edit most metadata elements for your In-App Purchases, except for the product ID and purchase type. If your In-App Purchase was never submitted to Apple for review, you can make changes to its metadata. However, if your In-App Purchase was already submitted, some changes require approval by App Review.

Learn how to modify In-App Purchases using the App Store Connect API.

Required role: Account Holder, Admin, or App Manager. View role permissions.

Note:

  • Developer has read-only access.

  • It may take up to 1 hour for changes you make to product metadata to appear in the sandbox environment.

Add and remove localizations

  1. In Apps, select the app you want to view.

  2. In the sidebar, under Monetization, click In-App Purchases. (For non-renewing subscriptions, click Subscriptions, then scroll down to Non-Renewing Subscriptions and click Manage.)

  3. Click the In-App Purchase you want to view or edit.

  4. Under App Store, in the Localization section, click Add Localization or the add button (+).

  5. To delete a localization, hold the pointer over the language listed under App Store Localizations, then click the remove button (—).

  6. Click Delete in the dialog.

Add or remove an Image

To promote your In-App Purchase on your App Store product page or to set up win-back offers, you must provide a promotional image. This image will represent your In-App Purchase in all countries or regions where your app is available. If you’re also using offer codes for auto-renewable subscriptions, your promotional image will appear on the App Store page when customers redeem their codes on iOS 14 or later. Your promotional image must meet the following requirements:

  • JPG or PNG

  • Dimensions of 1024 x 1024 pixels.

  • 72 dpi, RGB, flattened and no rounded corners.

To add or remove your image:

  1. In Apps, select the app you want to view.

  2. In the sidebar, under Monetization, click In-App Purchases. (For non-renewing subscriptions, click Subscriptions, then scroll down to Non-Renewing Subscriptions and click Manage.)

  3. Click the In-App Purchase you want to modify.

  4. Scroll down to the Image section.

  5. To add an image, click Choose File and upload the image. To delete an image, move your pointer over it and click the remove icon (—).

When you're ready, submit your In-App Purchase for review. Your Image must be approved by App Review before it will appear on the App Store.

Add App Review information

When submitting your In-App Purchase for review, include review notes and a screenshot to help Apple's review process. This information is for Apple’s review only and isn’t displayed on the App Store. You can change it before you submit to App Review, or if your In-App Purchase is rejected.

  1. In Apps, select the app you want to view.

  2. In the sidebar, under Monetization, click In-App Purchases. (For non-renewing subscriptions, click Subscriptions, then scroll down to Non-Renewing Subscriptions and click Manage.)

  3. Click the In-App Purchase you want to view or edit.

  4. Scroll down to the Review Information section.

  5. Click Choose File to upload your image.

  6. Add information under Review Notes, then click Save.
