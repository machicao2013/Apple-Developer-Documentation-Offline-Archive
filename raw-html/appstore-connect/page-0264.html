Manage your team

View and edit your profile

You can view and edit your App Store Connect profile. If your Apple Account is associated with more than one account, you can switch between accounts.

View your profile

On the top right of any App Store Connect page, click your name, then click Edit Profile.

Set your preferred currency

Your preferred currency is the default currency displayed in App Store Connect when setting pricing for your app or In-App Purchase. It's for App Store Connect display purposes only and isn't the currency that displays for customers on the App Store or the currency you're paid in.

  1. On the top right corner of any App Store Connect page, click your name, and then select Edit Profile.

  2. Choose your preferred currency from the Preferred Currency menu, then click Save.

Switch to another user account

If your Apple Account is associated with more than one App Store Connect or iTunes Connect account, you can switch between accounts.

On the top right corner of any App Store Connect page, click your name, then choose a different account.

Set email notifications

You can customize the account-related notifications you receive.

  1. On the top right of any App Store Connect page, click your name, then click Edit Profile.

  2. Under Notifications, choose the regions you want to receive notifications for from the menu for each notification type. Choose World to include all regions. Some notification types don't offer the ability to choose regions. You can receive these for all regions by selecting the corresponding checkbox.

    Note: To remove all selected regions and stop receiving a notification, click the delete icon (x) next to the region names or deselect the checkbox.

  3. On the top right, click Save.

Leave a team

You can remove yourself from a team anytime. However, if you’re the Account Holder, you’re legally responsible for the membership and can’t leave until you transfer the Account Holder role to someone else.

  1. On the top right of any App Store Connect page, click your name, then click Edit Profile. Ensure you’re viewing the account linked to the team you want to leave—if not, switch to the correct one.

  2. Click Leave Team, then confirm in the dialog.
