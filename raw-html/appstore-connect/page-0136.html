Remove app previews or screenshots

You can remove screenshots and app previews only when the app status is editable.

Learn about deleting App Screenshots and App Previews using the App Store Connect API.

Required role: Account Holder, Admin, App Manager, or Marketing. View role permissions.

  1. In Apps, select the app you want to view.

  2. In the sidebar, click the app version under the platform you wish to select.

  3. In the App Preview and Screenshots section, click View All Sizes in Media Manager.

  4. Select the appropriate device tab, then scroll to the device size where you want to delete the app preview or screenshots.

    If you want to remove it for a specific localization, on the top right, choose the language from the pop-up menu.

  5. Hold the pointer over the screenshot or app preview that you want to delete, and then click on the Delete button (–).

    Once the screenshot or app preview is deleted, it’ll also be deleted in all other locations.
