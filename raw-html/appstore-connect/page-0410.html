Definition

Billing Issue

Price Increase Notice

Subscriber canceled their subscription from the price increase notice.

Price Increase Consent

Subscription automatically ended because the price of the subscription increased and the subscriber did not consent.

Canceled

Subscriber canceled their subscription.

Removed From Sale

Subscription was removed from sale, by the developer or Apple.

Other

Subscription ended for another reason not listed above.
