Test iPhone and iPad apps on Macs with Apple silicon

You can test iPhone and iPad apps on Macs with Apple silicon using TestFlight. This functionality is set at the group level and applies to all builds within the tester group.

To enable testing for a tester group:

  1. In Apps, select the app you want to view.

  2. Click the TestFlight tab.

  3. In the sidebar, select the group you want to enable for testing.

  4. Click the settings tab within the group, and under Test iPhone and iPad apps on Apple silicon Macs, click Enable.

    Minimum macOS version compatibility is set in the Apple Silicon Mac Availability section in Pricing and Availability or the LSMinimumSystemVersion key in your build if you have opted out of making your app available on the Mac App Store.
