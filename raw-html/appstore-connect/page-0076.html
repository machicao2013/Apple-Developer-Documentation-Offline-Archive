Get started

App Store Connect homepage

You can access all sections of App Store Connect from the homepage. Each section allows you to access only the functionality related to your user role.

Click App Store Connect at the top of any page to return to the homepage at any time.

Note: The language settings on your browser or device affect the language in which App Store Connect appears to you.

Section

Description

Apps: Add apps to your account, edit app information, create new versions, and submit your app for review. Configure features and services, such as Game Center, In-App Purchase, and TestFlight.

App Analytics: Gain insights into your app's performance on the App Store in areas related to user acquisition, engagement, and monetization.

Learn about App Analytics.

Sales and Trends: View reports showing sales and trends over time.

Learn about Sales and Trends.

Payments and Financial Reports: View and download your monthly financial reports and payment information from the App Store, which includes your earnings, amounts owed, and last payment.

Learn about Payments and Financial Reports.

Business: Sign agreements and download agreements. Enter your tax information and set up electronic banking information to receive earnings payments from Apple.

Users and Access: Add users, delete users, create sandbox testers, change user roles, change user notifications, manage API keys, and manage access to the source code for Xcode Cloud.
