Configure Game Center

Manage scores and players

You can view and manage the top 100 scores and players for Game Center leaderboards with Live status. This helps you maintain a fair playing environment by deleting fake scores or blocking players that engage in fraudulent behavior.

You can restore scores and players that were removed within the last 30 days. To restore scores and players that were removed more than 30 days ago, contact us.

Required role: Account Holder, Admin, or App Manager. View role permissions.

Block a player

Block a player in order to remove their existing scores from any games using the leaderboard and block any future score submissions.

  1. From Apps, select your app.

  2. In the sidebar, click Game Center.

  3. In the Leaderboards, Leaderboard sets, or Group Leaderboards section, click the ellipsis menu (…) menu.

  4. Choose Manage Scores and Players.

  5. Find the leaderboard you want to manage in the Live Leaderboards section, then click Manage. This will display the top 100 scores for the selected leaderboard.

  6. In the Top Scores section, find the player’s name, then click Block.

  7. Click Block.

Unblock a player

When you unblock a player, they’re allowed to submit scores to any game that uses the leaderboard.

Note: Only the last posted score prior to blocking will be restored.

  1. From Apps, select your app.

  2. In the sidebar, click Game Center.

  3. In the Leaderboards, Leaderboard sets, or Group Leaderboards section, click the ellipsis menu (…) menu.

  4. Choose Manage Scores and Players.

  5. In the Blocked Players (Last 30 days) section, click Restore.

  6. In the Top Scores section, find the player’s name, then click Block.

  7. Click Restore.

Remove a score

  1. From Apps, select your app.

  2. In the sidebar, click Game Center.

  3. In the Leaderboards, Leaderboard sets, or Group Leaderboards section, click ellipsis menu (…) menu.

  4. Choose Manage Scores and Players.

  5. Click Manage. This will display the top 100 scores for the selected leaderboard.

  6. In the Top Scores section, find the player’s name and the score you’d like to remove, then click Remove.

  7. Click Remove.

Restore a score

A restored score, if it's still a top score, will reappear in the Top Scores (Past 30 Days) section.

Note: Restored scores may take up to 20 minutes to reappear.

  1. From Apps, select your app.

  2. In the sidebar, click Game Center.

  3. In the Leaderboards, Leaderboard sets, or Group Leaderboards section, click the ellipsis menu (…) menu.

  4. Choose Manage Scores and Players.

  5. Click Manage.

  6. In the Removed Scores (Last 30 Days) section, click Restore.

  7. Click Restore.
