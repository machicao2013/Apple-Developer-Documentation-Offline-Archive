Manage your app's availability

Make a version unavailable for download

To address legal or usability issues in a previous app version, you can remove the version from the App Store.

If the status of the version is Ready for Distribution and the version has a legal or usability issue, you must submit an app update. Clearly describe the previous version’s issue in your submission. If you’re unable to submit an update, you must remove the app from the App Store.

Required role: Account Holder, Admin, or App Manager. View role permissions.

  1. In Apps, select the app you want to view.

  2. In the sidebar, click Pricing and Availability.

  3. At the bottom of the page, under the Last-Compatible Version Settings, select the available version.

    Note: Only versions submitted to the App Store will appear in this list.

  4. In the dialog that appears, deselect versions that shouldn’t be available on the App Store, and click Done.

  5. On the top right, click Save.

Once a legal or usability issue is resolved, choose which versions to make available as outlined in Step 4.
